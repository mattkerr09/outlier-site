Architecture

Ternary experts. Frozen base. Tiny overlay.

Each MoE layer adds ternary routed experts on top of an unmodified Qwen2.5 base. The overlay is the product; the base is a dependency you already have. The flagship recovery is a 15 KB alpha file.

△

Ternary experts

Expert weights constrained to {-1, 0, +1} with per-expert learned scaling. ~1.6 bits per weight. Stored as an overlay on a frozen Qwen2.5 base.

■

Frozen Qwen base

Pretrained Qwen2.5 runs unmodified as the shared full-precision anchor. Always active. Provides the quality floor; ternary experts add specialization via CAKLD distillation.

⇄

Sparse routing

Top-K routing per MoE layer activates a subset of experts per token. Compute scales with active parameters. 68B total, 32B active on the V3.3 flagship.

◇

Paged expert cache

Three-tier hot/warm/cold cache with cross-layer prefetch at 81.5% hit rate. Hot experts in RAM, cold experts stream from disk. 150B-total on workstation hardware.

✦

Alpha-fix recovery

Post-training recovery primitive. 280 per-expert scalar gates trained in 18 minutes recovered +1.61pp MMLU on 68B where a 68M-parameter LoRA regressed. 15 KB overlay.

⊞

128K context

YaRN-4x scaling on the 68B flagship passes 100% passkey retrieval and needle-in-haystack at 32K, 64K, and 128K. Verified on B200.
