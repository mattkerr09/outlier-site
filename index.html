03 / research

A second track: our own MoE.

Ternary-quantized Mixture-of-Experts, built as residual deltas over a frozen base.

The shipping app runs curated open-weights models today. In parallel, Outlier trains its own model family — 10B, 40B, 70B, and 150B — using ternary {−1, 0, +1} expert weights layered on top of a full-precision FFN. Three provisional patents filed.

When a scale validates against the RAM budget of a real Mac tier, it graduates into the shipping stack. Until then, research is research — and the product stays honest.
